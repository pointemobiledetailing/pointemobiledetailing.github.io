The Canal Community

Michigan's Most Boat-Dense City

No community in Metro Detroit has more boats per household than St. Clair Shores. The city's extensive canal system — stretching through neighborhoods from 9 Mile to 15 Mile Road — gives an estimated 3,000+ homes direct boat access to Lake St. Clair. At Jefferson Beach Marina and countless private docks along the canals, pontoons, fishing boats, and runabouts are a daily reality. Pointe Mobile Detailing's mobile boat service is purpose-built for this community: we come to your slip, your canal dock, or your driveway — no trailer required.

Our boat detailing covers hull wash, deck cleaning, glass cleaning, electronic surface cleaning, exterior wax, and in-depth drying — the same professional treatment used at fixed marina shops, delivered where your boat actually lives. At $8/ft for first-time SCS customers and $10/ft for returning, the math is straightforward: a 22-foot pontoon runs $176 for a first detail. We're built for the everyday boat owner in the canals, not just the weekend warrior with a sport cruiser.
