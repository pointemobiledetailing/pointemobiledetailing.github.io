Michigan's Riviera

Where the Lake Meets Your Driveway

St. Clair Shores has earned its nickname "Michigan's Riviera" — the city sits along Lake St. Clair with an intricate canal system that gives thousands of households direct water access. That waterfront lifestyle means SCS residents tend to take their vehicles seriously: the pickup trucks, family SUVs, and daily drivers that navigate the Nautical Mile on Jefferson Avenue and the neighborhoods from 9 Mile to 15 Mile Road deserve regular professional care. Pointe Mobile Detailing serves all of St. Clair Shores — we come to your driveway so you don't need to interrupt your day.

The presence of Selfridge Air National Guard Base nearby brings a community that values discipline and vehicle upkeep. Whether you're a base resident, a family in the canals, or a professional driving the Jefferson corridor daily, we offer the full range from a $95 Exterior Clean to the $275 Pointe Elite. We schedule same-week appointments and call approximately one hour before arrival to confirm. Phone: 313-686-3911.
