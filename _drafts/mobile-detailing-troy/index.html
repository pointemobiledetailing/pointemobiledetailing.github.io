The Somerset Corridor

Luxury Detailing at Your Office

Troy is one of Metro Detroit's most affluent cities and a hub for corporate headquarters, law firms, and financial services. The Big Beaver Road corridor — running east from I-75 past the Somerset Collection to Crooks Road and beyond — is lined with office campuses where professionals park European sedans, luxury SUVs, and performance cars all day. Pointe Mobile Detailing is designed for exactly this customer: we come to your parking structure or surface lot while you're already at work and return your car detailed by the time your meeting ends.

Troy residents tend to drive vehicles that merit real care — BMWs, Audis, Lexus, and Mercedes are common in the corporate lots and residential neighborhoods near Wattles Road and Coolidge Highway. Our Pointe Elite package at $275 is the complete treatment for these vehicles: clay bar exterior, full hand wash, tire and wheel detail, windows, deep interior clean, leather conditioning, and carpet shampoo. For maintenance between full details, the Signature Shine at $185 handles exterior and interior in about two hours. We cover all of Troy and the surrounding Oakland County communities.
