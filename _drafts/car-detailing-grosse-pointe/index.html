Grosse Pointe's Five Communities

Grosse Pointe's five distinct communities — Grosse Pointe Park, Grosse Pointe Woods, Grosse Pointe Farms, Grosse Pointe Shores, and the City of Grosse Pointe — are among Metro Detroit's most established and affluent neighborhoods. Jefferson Avenue and the waterfront corridor are home to some of the region's finest vehicles: late-model BMWs, Range Rovers, Cadillacs, and Mercedes are the norm. When your car lives in a Grosse Pointe driveway, it deserves care that matches — and Pointe Mobile Detailing comes directly to you, no drop-off required.

We detail cars throughout every Grosse Pointe community, including along Lake Shore Drive near the Grosse Pointe Yacht Club, in The Village shopping district, and in the historic neighborhoods surrounding the Edsel Ford estate. Whether you're preparing a luxury sedan for a weekend outing or maintaining a daily driver that faces Michigan winters and road salt, our team brings professional equipment and premium products to your door. Same-week appointments are typically available, and we call ahead approximately one hour before arrival.
