About Us

Metro Detroit's Premier Mobile Detailing

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does mobile car detailing cost in Metro Detroit?

Exterior Clean starts at $95, Interior Refresh at $135, Signature Shine at $185, and Pointe Elite at $275. Boat detailing is $10/ft ($8/ft introductory rate for first-time customers). Pricing is confirmed within 1 hour of booking.

Do you detail boats in Metro Detroit?

Yes. We offer mobile boat detailing at your dock, driveway, or storage facility anywhere in Metro Detroit. Pricing is $10/ft ($8/ft for first-time customers). Services include hull wash, deck cleaning, glass cleaning, wax, and in-depth drying.

Do I need to provide water and electricity?

Yes — customers provide access to a water spigot and a power outlet at the service location. If you have questions about accommodations, discuss them at booking.

Do you offer same-day bookings?

Yes, same-day bookings are accepted based on availability. We call approximately 1 hour before arrival to confirm details.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Grosse Pointe, Grosse Pointe Park, Grosse Pointe Woods, Grosse Pointe Farms, Grosse Pointe Shores, Warren, Sterling Heights, Troy, Birmingham, Royal Oak, Southfield, Livonia, Dearborn, St. Clair Shores, Harper Woods, and Detroit.

What is your satisfaction guarantee?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you are not fully satisfied, contact us within 24 hours and we will return to address any concerns at no additional charge.
