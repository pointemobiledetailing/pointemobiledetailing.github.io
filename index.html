About Us

Metro Detroit's Premier Mobile Detailing

Pointe Mobile Detailing LLC is Metro Detroit's premier mobile car detailing service, bringing professional exterior and interior detailing directly to customers' homes and driveways across Grosse Pointe, Warren, Troy, Birmingham, and surrounding communities — no drop-off, no waiting.

Backed by our 100% customer satisfaction guarantee, every service is performed with care, precision, and a commitment to exceeding your expectations. We use only the finest pH-balanced soaps, microfiber towels, and professional-grade equipment on every vehicle.
