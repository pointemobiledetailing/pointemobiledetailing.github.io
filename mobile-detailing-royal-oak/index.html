The Urban Dog Owner's Detailer

Mobile Detailing Without a Garage

Royal Oak is one of Metro Detroit's most walkable and dog-friendly cities. Red Oaks Dog Park, Meininger Park, and the Woodward corridor draw dog owners year-round — and with them come the inevitable signs of pet ownership inside the car: embedded hair in seats and carpets, muddy paw prints, and that persistent doggy smell that a drive-through wash won't touch. Our Interior Refresh is the most requested service from Royal Oak customers: $135 for full steam cleaning, deep vacuum, carpet spot treatment, and odor elimination, brought directly to wherever your car is parked.

The practical reality of urban Royal Oak — houses with small driveways or on-street parking only — is exactly why mobile detailing works here. We don't need a garage or extra space; we bring our own water, power, and equipment. Whether you're in a walkable neighborhood near Woodward or in one of the neighborhoods on the south or east side of the city, we can reach you. The Signature Shine at $185 covers both interior and exterior in about two hours and is a popular choice for residents who want a comprehensive clean without blocking their entire Saturday.
