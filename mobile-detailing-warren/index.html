Macomb County's Automotive Capital

Where Auto Workers Get Their Cars Detailed

Warren is the industrial and automotive backbone of Macomb County — home to the General Motors Technical Center on Mound Road, one of the most significant automotive research campuses in the world. The workers, engineers, and managers who fill the streets on Van Dyke, 8 Mile, 10 Mile, and 12 Mile corridors tend to take vehicle quality seriously. That makes professional detailing a natural fit. Pointe Mobile Detailing brings full service detailing directly to Warren driveways — no drop-off, no waiting, no wasted Saturday.

Many Warren vehicles are daily drivers — crew cab trucks, family SUVs, and sedans that put on miles year-round and accumulate Michigan road salt, winter grime, and interior wear. Our $95 Exterior Clean is the practical maintenance wash for Warren's working drivers: a foam cannon pre-soak, full hand wash, wheel scrub, and towel dry. When it's time for a full refresh, the Signature Shine at $185 includes exterior, wheels, windows, and full interior wipe-down. We serve all of Warren and the surrounding Macomb County communities.
