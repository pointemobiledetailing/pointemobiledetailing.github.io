The Woodward Corridor

Where Cars Are Part of the Culture

Every August, Woodward Avenue becomes the center of the automotive world during the Woodward Dream Cruise — the largest single-day classic car event in the country. Birmingham sits squarely in this corridor, adjacent to Bloomfield Hills and surrounded by some of Metro Detroit's most valuable residential real estate. The people who live here tend to own vehicles worth caring for: classic and collector cars that need seasonal preparation, luxury SUVs, and performance vehicles that deserve better than a coin-operated touchless wash.

Pointe Mobile Detailing serves Birmingham and the surrounding Bloomfield Hills area. Our clay bar treatment in the Pointe Elite package is purpose-built for collector car owners who need paint decontamination before a show or the end of a storage season. We also serve daily drivers who simply want their car to look the way Birmingham deserves — clean, polished, and maintained. We come to your driveway or garage, bring all equipment, and work without disrupting your day. Same-week appointments, 1-hour arrival call.
